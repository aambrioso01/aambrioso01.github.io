Shysta

My path to songwriting isn't one of grand destiny or childhood aspirations, but one of eventual necessity. It all started in the 9th grade, rewriting Lil Wayne verses. After a couple years of slapping together shitty remixes and being unsatisfied with my day to day existence, I finally bought a mic in 2015. Writing and recording vocals scratched a creative itch that I had been sorely neglecting. Nowadays producing music helps me drain all that pesky angst that inevitably comes when boy meets world. Whether it's setting up mic stands, tweaking mixes, or just bouncing around ideas with the boys, I try to make myself as useful as possible to the musicians around me. Before anything, I'm a young kid with the age old aspiration of rockstardom. Whether my hobby becomes my career is a matter of interstellar probability; but even when I had no fans, I still looked to a notebook and pen for that sweet, sweet release.

Audio engineers
